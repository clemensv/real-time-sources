## Summary
Two candidates covering global coral bleaching monitoring (NOAA CRW) and Australia/GBR-specific marine monitoring (AIMS). NOAA Coral Reef Watch is the world's primary satellite-based bleaching heat stress system with daily global coverage. AIMS provides in-situ measurements from the Great Barrier Reef including real-time weather stations and periodic reef health surveys. Both use ERDDAP for data access. The coral/marine domain is characterized by scientific data formats (NetCDF) and mixed freshness (daily satellite vs. periodic in-situ surveys).
