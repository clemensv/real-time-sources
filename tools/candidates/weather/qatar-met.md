## What It Provides

Qatar's meteorological department monitors weather for a small but strategically important Gulf state (2.9M people). Qatar experiences extreme summer heat (50°C+), occasional dust storms, and rare but intense rainfall that causes flash flooding in the flat desert terrain.

Key aspects:
- **Extreme heat monitoring**: Qatar's 2022 FIFA World Cup was moved to winter due to summer heat
- **Aviation weather**: Doha Hamad International is a major global hub (ICAO: OTHH)
- **Marine weather**: Persian Gulf conditions for LNG tankers (Qatar is world's largest LNG exporter)
- **Dust storms**: Shamal wind events
