## Overview

Iraq's railway system is limited and under reconstruction after decades of conflict and neglect:
- **Iraqi Republic Railways (IRR)** — state-owned operator
- **Baghdad-Basra line** — main route, passenger service resumed in 2015
- **Baghdad-Mosul line** — under reconstruction (damaged during ISIS occupation)
- **International** — connections to Turkey (planned), Iran (exists but infrequent)

Passenger rail in Iraq is **minimal** compared to bus and air travel. Frequency:
- Baghdad-Basra: a few trains per day
- Baghdad-Fallujah: commuter service
- International: weekly at best
