## Summary

Two candidates covering African disease surveillance. disease.sh provides structured JSON data for all African countries — verified live with COVID-19 statistics for South Africa, Kenya, Nigeria, Egypt, Ghana, and Ethiopia. However, it's community-maintained and currently COVID-focused. Africa CDC is the authoritative continental disease surveillance body tracking mpox, cholera, Ebola, measles, and more, but lacks a machine-readable API — publications are HTML/PDF. The disease surveillance domain in Africa is high-value but low-accessibility. WHO AFRO and ProMED may provide complementary data pathways.
