## Summary

Three agriculture-focused candidates for Africa. The FAO Desert Locust Information Service monitors locust swarms across the Sahel and Horn of Africa through an ArcGIS Hub — the 2019–2021 East Africa crisis was the worst in 70 years. Copernicus NDVI provides dekadal vegetation health monitoring at 300m resolution, the foundation for crop condition assessment. iSDA provides continental soil maps at 30m resolution — static but invaluable for agricultural context. Together with CHIRPS rainfall and FEWS NET food security data, these form a complete agricultural early warning chain for Africa.
